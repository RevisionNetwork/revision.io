The Transparency Database is an online platform for searching, comparing, propagating and negotiating ethical technology principles and related organizational objectives. 
**
The database serves as a transparency tool for organizations to adopt goals, showcase their progress, find overlapping areas for research with other stakeholders, build inter-organizational and cross-border solutions and establish a process of constant reassessment of tension areas. 
**
It also serves as a platform for experts, researchers, the media and everyone who wants to raise concerns, highlighting tension areas and follow organizational commitments to ethical technology goals.

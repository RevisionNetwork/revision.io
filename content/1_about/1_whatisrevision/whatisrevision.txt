Title: Purpose

----

Text:

<p>REVISION is a shared, global accountability network of individuals, institutions and organizations committed to building a future where we all collectively thrive.</p>

<p>Our network brings together the public and private sectors within a networked governance model for creating a safe space for innovation and responsible leadership.</p>

<p>Our members support each other to find shared mental models, build interorganizational solutions and establish a process of constant negotiation and reassessment of individual, collective and societal goals.</p>

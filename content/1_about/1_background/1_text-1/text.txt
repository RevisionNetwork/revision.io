We face times when our societies are as volatile as our ecologies are disturbed. Capital is growing without any regard for human and planetary well-being. Meanwhile, new technologies are evolving faster than our ideological capacity to map out their long-term consequences.
##
When the market economy is extractive and public regulation is too slow, we need a new regulatory system that is able to act across borders and accommodate the need for rapid adaptation.

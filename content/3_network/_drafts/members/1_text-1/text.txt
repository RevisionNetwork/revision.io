## Individual

Individual change-agents join the NETWORK to be part of multistakeholder expert groups, study circles, and systemic intervention projects, keep up to date with organizational transparency goals, provide feedback, and be part of global interorganizational sustainability initiatives.

#### Benefits:

-  Meet like-minded experts
-  Join P2P feedback groups and support other leaders
-  Grow skills & knowledge in the network
-  Support the realization of interorganizational sustainability goals
-  Have a real impact

##3 layers of our accountability mechanism:
###
- TRANSPARENCY: collecting and publishing information through the searchable public Transparency Database
- PROOF: real-time updates with certified data 
- ANSWERABILITY: actions and decisions are made between equally privileged, equipotent participants in a networked model that fosters a culture of accountability

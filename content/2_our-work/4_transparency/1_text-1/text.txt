Text: The Transparency Database is an online platform for searching, comparing, propagating and negotiating organizational goals organized under the Ethical Technology Principles. The database serves as a transparency tool for organizations to adopt goals, showcase their progress (Key Results), find overlapping areas for research with other stakeholders, build inter-organizational and cross-border solutions and establish a process of constant reassessment and negotiation of tension areas. It also serves as a resource for experts and anyone interested in raising concerns, highlighting tension areas and following organizational commitments to ethical technology.

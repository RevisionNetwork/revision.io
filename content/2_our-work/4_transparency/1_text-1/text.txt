The Transparency Database is an online platform for searching, comparing, propagating and negotiating ethical technology principles. 
**
The database serves as a transparency tool for organizations to adopt goals, showcase their progress (Key Results), find overlapping areas for research with other stakeholders, build inter-organizational and cross-border solutions and establish a process of constant reassessment of tension areas. 
**
It also serves as a platform for experts, researchers, the media and everyone who is ready to raise concerns, highlighting tension areas and follow organizational commitments in the field of ethical technology.
